ABOUT US


Hi! We are Sargam, Khadyja, Hailie, and Zoya, the team that makes up CLINICAL.
We are so excited for you to enjoy this website that we have created.
Our team truly believes that the freedom of expression offered through real fashion can aid people in keeping a positive mindset and healthy mentality.
While there is much work to be done in the fashion industry in terms of inclusivity and equality, we are passionate advocates of fashion designers, brands, and models that work to make people aware of mental health problems due to body image and inclusivity and eradicate these problems in the industry, little by little.
Fashion has the potential to make people feel truly good about themselves because...


when you look good, you feel good.
